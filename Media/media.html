Teaser and the Firecat Super Deluxe Box Set

Yusuf / Cat Stevens celebrates 1971’s landmark album with this extensive 4CD and Blu-Ray 50th anniversary collection. Includes 41 previously unreleased demos, sessions and live tracks and 21 previously unreleased TV performances.

Tea For The Tillerman²

50 years after the release of the original era-defining album, T4TT2 sees Yusuf reimagine the same eleven songs for a new age with dramatic results. T4TT2 is released on September 18th 2020.

Mona Bone Jakon Super Deluxe Box Set

Half a century after its original release, the Mona Bone Jakon 50th anniversary box set represents the definitive version of the album. Featuring 24 previously unreleased songs, an entire CD of unheard studio demos, and a range of collectable ephemera.

Tea For The Tillerman Super Deluxe Box Set

Half a century after its release, the 50th anniversary Super Deluxe Collector’s Edition box set represents the definitive collection relating to one of the most beloved albums of all time.

The Laughing Apple

The Laughing Apple features newly written songs and re-recordings of some of Yusuf’s early material, allowing him to present the songs as he’s always wished they’d been recorded.

Tell ‘Em I’m Gone

Tell ‘Em I’m Gone was produced with the legendary Rick Rubin and sees Yusuf explore some of his bluesier influences.

Roadsinger

Following 2006’s Another Cup, Roadsinger showed that Yusuf / Cat’s return to making music had not been a fleeting venture.

An Other Cup

In 2006 Cat, now Yusuf, delighted fans and the music industry with the release of An Other Cup, his first major release since 1978’s Back to Earth.

Back To Earth

Back to Earth would become Cat’s last album for almost 30 years. By the time of its release he had embraced the Muslim faith and changed his name to Yusuf Islam.

Izitiso

Having departed from his traditional singer-songwriter style for 1975’s Numbers, Izitso saw Cat return to a style that was more in keeping with the Tillerman, Teaser albums.

Numbers

Similarly to the way in which 1973’s Foreigner saw Cat explore the influence that R&B had had upon him, Numbers drew upon his love of musicals.

Prayers Of The Last Prophet

The follow up to 1995’s The Life of the Last Prophet, Prayers of the Last Prophet is a collection of duas as used by the prophet Muhammad (peace be upon him).
